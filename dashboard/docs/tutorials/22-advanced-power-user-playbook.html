Tutorial: Advanced Multi-Feature Combos & Power User Playbook

Context

What I Need You To Do

Strategy 1: The Complete Growth Engine

Goal: Grow from 0 to 10K followers systematically.

Phase A: Foundation (Week 1)

1. Set up profile perfectly:
   ‚Üí x_get_profile (audit current profile)
   ‚Üí x_update_profile (optimize bio, avatar, header)
   
2. Research your niche:
   ‚Üí x_get_trends (what's trending)
   ‚Üí x_search_tweets "niche topic" (find active communities)
   ‚Üí x_analyze_sentiment (understand niche mood)
   
3. Identify targets:
   ‚Üí x_get_followers @competitor1 (see who follows competitors)
   ‚Üí x_get_followers @competitor2
   ‚Üí Export and analyze overlap

Phase B: Content + Engagement (Weeks 2-4)

Daily routine via Claude:
  Morning:
    ‚Üí x_get_trends (spot opportunities)
    ‚Üí x_post_tweet (share insight while it's fresh)
    ‚Üí x_auto_like keywords=["niche term"] limit=20
    
  Midday:
    ‚Üí x_search_tweets "niche question" (find people asking questions)
    ‚Üí x_reply to 5-10 people with helpful answers
    ‚Üí x_follow 10-15 new relevant people
    
  Evening:
    ‚Üí x_get_analytics (check what performed)
    ‚Üí x_get_post_analytics (which posts worked)
    ‚Üí Plan tomorrow's content based on data

Phase C: Scale (Weeks 5-12)

  Weekly workflow:
    ‚Üí x_workflow_run "Growth Pipeline" (automated daily tasks)
    ‚Üí x_stream_start type="mention" (catch every mention)
    ‚Üí x_reputation_report period="7d" (weekly sentiment check)
    ‚Üí x_competitor_analysis handles=["@comp1","@comp2"]
    ‚Üí Adjust strategy based on data
    
  Bi-weekly cleanup:
    ‚Üí x_get_non_followers (find who doesn't follow back)
    ‚Üí x_unfollow_non_followers (maintain healthy ratio)
    ‚Üí x_detect_unfollowers (track who left)

Phase D: Optimize & Compound (Month 3+)

  Content optimization:
    ‚Üí Analyze top 20 performing tweets
    ‚Üí Identify winning formats, topics, hooks, times
    ‚Üí Create content templates
    ‚Üí x_post_thread (long-form on winning topics)
    ‚Üí x_schedule_post (queue content at optimal times)
    
  Community building:
    ‚Üí x_get_list_members (monitor niche lists)
    ‚Üí Engage consistently with same people
    ‚Üí Build relationships via x_send_dm (genuine outreach)

Strategy 2: Brand Reputation Command Center

Goal: 24/7 brand monitoring with instant response capability.

Setup (one-time):
  1. x_monitor_reputation target="@mybrand" interval=300 webhookUrl="slack_url"
  2. x_monitor_reputation target="brand name" type="keyword" interval=600
  3. x_stream_start type="mention" username="mybrand" interval=30
  4. x_workflow_create name="Brand Alert Pipeline"

Daily operations:
  Morning check:
    ‚Üí x_reputation_report period="24h"
    ‚Üí x_get_notifications limit=50
    ‚Üí x_analyze_sentiment texts=[...overnight_mentions]
    ‚Üí Prioritize responses for negative mentions
    
  Response workflow:
    ‚Üí For each negative mention:
      ‚Üí x_reply (empathetic response)
      ‚Üí Track in CRM
    ‚Üí For positive mentions:
      ‚Üí x_like + x_retweet (amplify positive voices)
      ‚Üí x_reply (thank them)
    
  End of day:
    ‚Üí x_reputation_report period="24h" format="markdown"
    ‚Üí Compare to yesterday: improving or declining?
    ‚Üí Adjust strategy

    ‚Üí Weekend: Engagement (reply + like marathon)
  
  3. Schedule:
    ‚Üí x_schedule_post for each planned tweet
    ‚Üí Optimal times from analytics data

  ‚Üí Reply to 10 comments on your posts
  ‚Üí Engage with 5 accounts in your niche
  ‚Üí x_get_post_analytics (real-time performance check)

  ‚Üí x_search_tweets "alternative to [competitor]"

  1. x_get_profile (review your public presence)
  2. x_get_following (who are you following?)
  3. x_get_non_followers (who doesn't follow back?)
  4. x_get_blocked (review block list)
  5. x_get_settings (check privacy settings)

  1. x_unfollow_non_followers (clean follow ratio)
  2. Remove spammy followers (block + unblock)
  3. x_clear_bookmarks (declutter)
  4. Content cleanup: unlike old tweets, delete irrelevant posts
  5. x_toggle_protected (if needed for privacy)

Backup:
  ‚Üí x_export_account (full backup)
  ‚Üí Store locally
  ‚Üí Set up weekly backup workflow
